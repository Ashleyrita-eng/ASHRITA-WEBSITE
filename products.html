Feed Formulation Ingredients

We supply quality feed raw materials used in the formulation of nutritious livestock and poultry feeds. Our ingredients are carefully sourced to support healthy growth, productivity, and balanced animal nutrition.

Maize Germ

Maize germ is an excellent source of energy and is widely used in livestock and poultry feed formulations. It supports growth, weight gain, and overall animal productivity.

Energy Source

Wheat Bran

Wheat bran is rich in fibre and energy, helping improve digestion and feed utilization. It is commonly used in cattle, poultry, pig, and other livestock feeds.

Fibre Rich

Wheat Pollard

Wheat pollard is a valuable feed ingredient that provides both energy and protein. It is highly palatable and contributes to balanced animal nutrition.

Balanced

Sunflower Seed Cake

Sunflower seed cake is a protein-rich ingredient obtained after oil extraction from sunflower seeds. It supports muscle development, growth, and improved animal performance.

Protein Rich

Soya Meal

Soya meal is one of the highest quality plant protein sources used in feed formulation. It contains essential amino acids necessary for healthy growth and production.

Premium Protein

Cotton Seed Cake

Cotton seed cake is a nutritious protein supplement commonly used in dairy and beef cattle feeds. It helps improve body condition and overall productivity.

Cattle Feed

Fish Meal

Fish meal is a premium protein source rich in amino acids, vitamins, and minerals. It is particularly valuable in poultry, fish, and young animal feed formulations.

Premium

Limestone

Limestone is an important source of calcium used in feed formulation. It supports strong bones, healthy growth, and improved eggshell quality in laying birds.

Calcium Source
