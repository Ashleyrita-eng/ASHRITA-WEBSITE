Fertilized Eggs - Quality Breeding Stock

✓ What Are Fertilized Eggs?

Fertilized eggs are premium quality eggs suitable for hatching, containing viable embryos ready to develop into healthy chicks under proper incubation conditions.

✓ Key Benefits

Higher hatchability rates • Genetic diversity for breeding • Cost-effective flock expansion • Quality assurance from trusted suppliers

✓ Proper Storage

Store at 55-65°F with 75% humidity. Turn regularly and use within 10 days before incubation for best results.

✓ Incubation Success

Maintain consistent temperature (99.5°F), proper humidity, and regular egg turning. Our premium feeds and supplements ensure strong chick development.
